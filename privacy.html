Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Ekantik Capital Advisors LLC ("ECA," "we," "us") collects information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Schedule a consultation through our booking system (Calendly)
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or other channels
  • Use our interactive calculator tools on this website

This may include your name, email address, phone number, and financial information you choose to share during consultations.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Provide personalized financial strategy recommendations
  • Communicate about our services and founding member program
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our business (scheduling, communication tools)
  • Regulatory bodies as required by law
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting) as needed for business operations

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@ekantikcapital.com.

Calculator Data

Information entered into our interactive calculators is processed entirely in your browser. No calculator inputs are transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Cookies and Analytics

This website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
